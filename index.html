Mobile Robot for IoT and Social Cyber-Physical Systems

Jarvis is an important agent in our Smart Building IoT system in which it moves around the building to collect sensor data such as illuminance, temperature, humidity, air quality at various spatial locations, perform analyses on collected data and control the IoT devices such as lighting, curtains, air-conditioner in the building to offer comfortable and eco-friendly indoor environment for humans to work in.
